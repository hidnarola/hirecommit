No More Candidate Back-outs,
No More Last Minute Surprises

Solve the problem of candidates backing out after accepting offer, use the path-breaking HireLock feature to enhance joining rates. HireLock offer can be designed to suit candidate profile. Choose from a one-way option for candidates to commit, or A two-way option enables both the company and candidate to commit. HireLock can be turned off or set for individual offers.

Candidates who accept HireLock offer commit to join when they accept offer and give consent that if they back out their information made available to future prospective employers.
